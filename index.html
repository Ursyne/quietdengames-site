About Quiet Den Games

Quiet Den Games is a one-person studio for small, focused experiments: short sessions, specific moods, and systems that lean a little strange. Projects skew narrative and atmospheric, but they are always trying a new shape, whether that is a phone call that loops wrong or a board of shifting tiles.
