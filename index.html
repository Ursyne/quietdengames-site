The Interval Bureau: Extension 0

Every choice is logged. Every pause is assessed. A 45-minute branching audio narrative where workplace protocol meets cosmic intrusion. Navigate strange menus, enforced breathing exercises, and controlled scenarios that bend cause and effect. The Interval Bureau is listening.

Play on itch.io Press & details
Audio-first. One desk, one phone, no clutter. Single sitting. Built to be finished in one go. Comfort warning. Breath control, surveillance, depersonalization.

Experience

Extension 0 is focused: a short, contained call that goes wrong in controlled steps. No filler, no grind, just a line that evaluates how you respond when the script fractures.

Branching audio The story lives in voice, sound design, and your numbered responses.
Corporate horror Protocol, compliance, and monitoring instead of cheap shock.
Minimalist presentation Static framing on the phone keeps focus where it belongs: the call.
Multiple paths Different routes surface different layers of the Interval Bureau.

About Quiet Den Games

Quiet Den Games is a one-person label making concise, atmospheric interactive work: tight runtimes, deliberate writing, and systems that stay out of the way.

Extension 0 is the first public release set inside the Interval Bureau, with room to expand if it earns it.
