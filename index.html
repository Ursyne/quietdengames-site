The Interval Bureau: Extension 0

A ~45-minute branching audio narrative about a corporate help line that evaluates your breathing, your pauses, and your ability to follow instructions when they stop making sense.

Every choice is logged. Every pause is assessed. The call will be completed. The only question is what it completes into.

What it is

Extension 0 is a tightly scoped, replayable narrative built to be played in one sitting. You navigate a corporate hotline that slowly abandons its script. The systems are simple. The tension comes from what the line asks of you and how it responds when you hesitate.

Branching audio narrative No dialogue trees on screen. Your input is keys and timing. The story lives in the audio.
Corporate horror, not gore Surveillance language, protocol worship, and controlled breathing. Unsettling without cheap jumps.
Minimalist visuals A single desk. A phone. The dark around it. Enough to frame the call, nothing to distract from it.
Multiple paths, one file Different routes reveal different fragments of the Interval Bureau and its "Arbiter".

How to play

  • Use number keys to respond to prompts (e.g. press 1, 2, 3).
  • Some inputs only register during the last seconds of a message; the call tells you when.
  • Breathing prompts are fictional. Ignore them if they feel uncomfortable.
  • The line tracks how you cooperate, stall, or disobey. Most paths are valid. A few are not.

Content notes: breath control themes, panic sensations, depersonalization, corporate surveillance, intrusive language. No graphic violence.
