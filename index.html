About the studio

Small games. Dense moods. No wasted runtime.

Quiet Den Games is a one-person studio making interactive work with a sharp edge: compact projects, strong atmosphere, and writing that knows when to whisper instead of explain.

The catalog grows through experiments rather than formulas. Most releases lean narrative, all of them chase a distinct feeling, and each one is trying to do at least one thing it has not done before.
